CHILD SPONSORSHIP PROJECT

Child Smile Foundation runs this project on behalf of the children in our school communities whose parents or guardians are not capable to pay school fees for their children because of poverty, unemplyoiment, sudden disabilities or chronic sickness or death. You can easily notice many children not attending school in this area not because they don't want to study but simply because there is no body to pay school fees and other requirements. This project will help such children to get a sponsor or group sponsors to support their education.

Anyone can be a sponsor provided one has a heart for the children and a heart to support children in their different trials. Many years ago, children used to be safe and could not know the challenges of life not until they have reached a certain mature age, that could allow them to see life and its associated challenges, but today's generation is very different, children come into the world and few years later, they start experiencing the hardness of life, for many children, this starts at a very early age like many of those on our list below.

CHILD SELECTION FOR SPONSORSHIP

You can scroll down the list and sellect the child you would like to sponsor, a boy or a girl, you can select one or two or more depending on your financial circumstances. Love for the children and the willingness to support them acquire knowledge and understanding is a key factor. Once you have decided on which one to sponsor, please go to our Contact Us page and send a short message regarding your choice and the period you would like to sponsor the child or children. The initial period for sponsoring a child should be at least one year, this means that the sponsor should have at least enough money to sponsor a child or children for at least a period of one year. This helps the sponsored child or children to be sure that he/she can spend the year in school.

CHILD SPONSORSHIP PACKAGES

Child Smile Foundation has the following packages for those willing to sponsor a child under our project, this provision helps people to fit in despite their financial possibilities. We believe anyone can sponsor a vulnerable child or an orphan in Uganda and give chance and opportunity for the sponsored child to explore his God given talents and potential to maximize on his chance to succeed and prosper in life.

We are appealing to everyone especially to those with heart for the children to support us and this project This will help many more children who are not currently attending school to be enrolled and start the learning process, necessary for the child's mental and physical development and pave way for him or her to succeed in life just like any other child who is cared for by parents.

The sponsor has the right to communicate with his/her child periodically to know how the child is doing and also to learn about the progress at school. This kind of communication will pass through the school administration and be delivered to the child. The child also will write a letter to thank the sponsor for the support he/she provides to the child to receive education in this regard.

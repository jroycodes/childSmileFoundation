BECOME A VOLUNTEER

We are actively seeking individuals who are willing to generously contribute their time and expertise to Child Smile Foundation. Our organization has several areas where volunteers can make a substantial impact:

Administrative Support: We need assistance in organizing our office files, ensuring efficient operations.

Digital Engagement: Volunteers who can regularly update our website are crucial for keeping our online presence informative and engaging.

Educational Support: Teaching English, Mathematics, and Science is of paramount importance for the children's future. Your guidance in these subjects can be transformative.

Skills Development: Hands-on skills, such as carpentry, tailoring, hair cutting, and braiding, are invaluable for equipping these children with practical abilities.

Hygiene Education: Specialized lessons on hygiene, especially tailored for young girls, are essential for their well-being.

SPONSOR A CHILD

Our organization extends an earnest appeal to all kind-hearted individuals to join hands in supporting vulnerable children on their educational journey. Currently, we are providing education to more than 500 children, and the need for support is evident. By sponsoring just one child, you can make a profound difference. Alternatively, you have the opportunity to sponsor a group of children, magnifying your impact. If you wish to become a sponsor at Mercy Nursery and Primary School, we wholeheartedly invite you to connect with us. Simply fill out our Contact Us form to express your willingness to support a child or a group of children in their pursuit of education. We eagerly await your contact and will provide you with all the necessary information to embark on this transformative journey of sponsorship. Your contribution can change lives today!

PARTNER WITH US

We invite you to participate in our fundraising initiatives, which directly impact the lives of children at Mercy Nursery and Primary School. You can contribute to various projects, each with the potential to create a positive change:

Classroom Construction: Help us build essential classrooms, providing a conducive learning environment.

Classroom Renovation: Support us in renovating classrooms, ensuring a vibrant and inspiring space for our students.

Teacher Sponsorship: Contribute to funding teachers' salaries. Our "equalizing" approach ensures that one child's fees support another child's education.

Meal Programs: Fundraise for meals for individual learners or groups.
